"Lets Connect and Build Africa."

AFRICA YOUTH SUMMIT 2022

Young people make up the bulk of Africa’s total population with an estimated 75% of the continent’s population below the age of 35. African Youth Summit is working with young leaders in all African countries and joining forces with civil society, companies and people on the frontline of climate change, education and skills improvement, health and well-being, good governance, human rights and accountability, employment opportunities and leadership skills to empower young leaders with the neccessary knowledge to develop and transform Africa into a better place.

Feel free to contact us about the African Youth Sumit via our email or incase you have any questions. africasummit@gmail.com
